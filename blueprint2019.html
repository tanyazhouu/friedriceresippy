how to cook fried rice (炒饭)

let's go!

some ingredients:

  • white, cooked rice
  • carrot
  • scallion
  • soy sauce
  • vegetable oil
  • egg
  • salt
  • any meat (if desired)

yummy necessities:

  • frying pan
  • spatula
  • bowl

time to actually make this stuff:

  1. crack and scramble eggs in a separate bowl
  2. chop vegetables (scallion and carrot)
  3. prepare the frying pan by pouring veggie oil
  4. dump rice into the pan, along with eggs and veggies
  5. stir, poke, and jostle rice around with spatula so that rice
    doesn't stick to pan
  6. keep cooking and add soy sauce and salt while stirring
  7. pour rice into bowls until eggs and rice are fully cooked,
    or until satisfied
